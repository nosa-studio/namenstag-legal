1. Overview

This page describes what data the Namenstag app by Nosa Studio collects and how you can request its deletion. Namenstag is designed to process as little personal data as possible: no user account is required, and the most important data (favorites and contact data) stays on your device.

2. What data does Namenstag collect?

2.1 Purchase and subscription data (RevenueCat)

When you subscribe or make a one-time purchase, the transaction is processed by RevenueCat, our subscription and billing provider. RevenueCat stores a customer ID, your purchase history, and your subscription status on its servers so that purchases can be restored across devices. We do not see or store your payment card details.

2.2 Anonymous usage analytics (PostHog)

The app uses PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics (e.g. which features are used, crash diagnostics). No personal identifiers are transmitted — no names, no email addresses, no contact lists, no precise location. PostHog assigns a randomly generated identifier per install, which is reset when the app's data is cleared or the app is uninstalled.

2.3 Favorites and contacts cache (local only)

Your favorites and the cache of contact names matched against the name-day calendar are stored only locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers and is automatically removed when you uninstall the app or clear the app's data in Android system settings.

3. How do I request deletion of my data?

3.1 Local on-device data

Favorites and the contacts cache live only on your device. You delete them immediately by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data in Android system settings. No request to us is required.

3.2 Server-side data (RevenueCat, PostHog)

To request deletion of data stored by RevenueCat or PostHog, send an email to support.nosastudio@gmail.com with the subject:

Data Deletion Namenstag

Please include one of the following in your message so we can locate your data:

  • your RevenueCat customer ID (found in the app under Settings), or
  • the email address used for the purchase on the Google Play Store.

We will process your request within 30 days, forward the deletion to our processors (RevenueCat and, if applicable, PostHog), and confirm completion to you by email.

4. Consequences of deletion

  • An active subscription will continue until the end of the billing period you have already paid for; payments already made are not refunded.
  • Once the RevenueCat customer ID is deleted, purchases can no longer be restored automatically on a new device.
  • For legal reasons (e.g. tax retention obligations), we may retain invoicing and transaction records for the period required by law.
